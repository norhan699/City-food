Spicy Chicken Pasta


How to make Spicy Chicken Breast

Take one chicken breast,Butterfly,Add spicy marinade,Rub into meat,Fry until nicely charred,Flip, fry then remove




Ingrediants


Spaghetti

7oz / 200g Linguine
(or other long cut pasta)

Salsa

6 (2oz/60g) Sun Dried
Tomatoes, finely diced

Spices

Salt & Pepper, to taste

Chicken

1x 9oz/250g Chicken Breast,
at room temp
